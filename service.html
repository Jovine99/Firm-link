Our Services

Premium Security Services

Risk & Threat Mitigation
We conduct in-depth security assessments and provide real-time, on-the-ground intelligence from a global network of security professionals, including former military personnel and field operatives.

Executive Protection
We offer high-profile executive protection services, both covert and overt, tailored to individual and organizational needs, leveraging our experience in high-risk areas.

Protective Surveillance
Covert security operations to protect individuals or assets discreetly, ideal for those who do not want visible security.

Search, Rescue and Evacuation
Specialized emergency response teams for disaster situations, political unrest, or personal evacuations, including medical and rescue services.

Residential Security
Full residential, estate, and office security management, including staff vetting, risk analysis, and security systems installation.

Security Consulting
Custom security solutions tailored to clients' needs, focusing on risk mitigation and practical security protocols for individuals, corporations, NGOs, and high-net-worth clients.

Embedded & Concierge Services
Provides embedded analysts for on-site intelligence and concierge-style reporting for specific security concerns.

Travel Security
Custom travel security services, including secure transportation, hotel assessments, and risk evaluation for domestic and international travel.

Counter-Terrorism Services
Counter-terrorism search teams and trained security dogs for high-risk areas or events, providing comprehensive protection from terrorism threats.

Event Security
Comprehensive event security planning, including risk analysis, crowd control, access management, and emergency response.

Aviation Security
Aviation security services including air marshal provision, crew and passenger escorts, and sensitive cargo protection.

Asset Protection
Secure transportation and protection of high-value assets, with tailored plans based on thorough risk assessments.

Operations Centre
A 24/7 operations center providing logistical support, incident response, and real-time intelligence monitoring.

Workplace Safety
Consulting and supervision to prevent workplace accidents and ensure health and safety compliance.
